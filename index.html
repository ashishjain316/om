Enhance the flavour of your food by using a variety of colours and shapes to entice the eyes

Taste the colours

Red

Red food remind us of berries and soft fruits, so we anticipate a sweet taste.
Green

Fresh, zingy green colours are reminiscent of unripe fruit, promisiong sour or acid flavours.
White

White foods evoke memories of salt and salty flavours, driving the expectation of a savoury treat.
